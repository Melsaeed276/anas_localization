Here's what the Flutter community thinks so far. 👇

5 days since we launched **anas_localization** on pub.dev — and already the numbers tell a story:

📦 **150 downloads** in the first week
⭐ **90 pub points** out of 140 (and climbing as we fix the last analysis flags)
🐛 **4 open GitHub issues** — which actually makes me happy

That last one might surprise you. Issues mean people installed it, tried it for real, and cared enough to report what they found. That's the best early signal a package author can get.

We're just getting started. The roadmap includes:
→ Pushing pub points to 130+
→ Adding more language coverage to the example app
→ CI/CD improvements based on the open issues

If you've tried anas_localization, I'd genuinely love a ⭐ on GitHub or a 👍 on pub.dev — it helps other Flutter devs discover the package and keeps the momentum going.

And if something broke or felt wrong: file an issue. That's how this gets better.

🔗 pub.dev: https://pub.dev/packages/anas_localization
🔗 GitHub: https://github.com/Melsaeed276/anas_localization

This package is named in memory of **Anas Al-Sharif**, a Palestinian journalist who gave his life reporting truth. Every download is a small Sadaqah Jariyah in his honor.
